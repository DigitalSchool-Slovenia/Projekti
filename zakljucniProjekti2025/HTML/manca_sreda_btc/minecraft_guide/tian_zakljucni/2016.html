Updates of year 2016!!

1.9 - The Combat Update - February 29, 2016

Additions include a new combat system, dual wielding, spectral and tipped arrows, and shields.

1.10 - The Frostburn Update - June 8, 2016

It added some new features such as skeleton/zombie variants in snowy biomes and deserts respectively, polar bears, structure blocks for saving/loading structures in worlds, and an auto-jump feature.

1.11 - The Exploration Update - November 14, 2016

This update made large changes to entity IDs, and added illagers (vindicators and evokers), vexes, totems of undying, exploration maps, observers, shulker boxes, woodland mansions and llamas.
