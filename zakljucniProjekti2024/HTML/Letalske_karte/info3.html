TEXSAS

When describing Texas, the term " six flags over Texas " is often used, referring to the several states that ruled the territory of present-day Texas. Spain was the first European country to own and control Texas. France had a short-lived colony , and then the territory was controlled by Mexico. On March 2, 1836, Texas declared independence and became the Republic of Texas . [1] In 1845, Texas joined the Union as the 28th state. [2] The annexation of Texas set in motion a series of events that led to the Mexican-American War of 1846. The state, which had been a slave state prior to the American Civil War , declared secession from the United States in early 1861 and seceded on March 2 of the same year. officially joined the Confederate States of America . After the Civil War and reintegration into the federal government, Texas entered a long period of economic stagnation.
