MEXSICO

The United States of Mexico , or Mexico for short ( Spanish : México , officially Estados Unidos Mexicanos ; the alternative spelling Méjico is sometimes used in Spain , but this is highly obsolete in the rest of the Spanish-speaking world) is a coastal state in North America , bordered to the north by The United States of America , to the southeast by Guatemala and Belize , to the west by the Pacific Ocean , and to the east by the Gulf of Mexico and the Caribbean Sea .

HISTORY

Mexico was the cradle of many developed Indian civilizations , such as the Mayans and Aztecs . The arrival of the Spanish in the 16th century and their conquest of the Aztec state in 1521 marked the beginning of Mexico's colonial era as part of New Spain. In 1810, Mexico declared independence from Spain , which after a long war led to independence in 1821. After independence, the territory of Mexico gradually shrank at the expense of territories that Mexico either sold to the United States or lost in the Mexican-American War , and account of the independence of the Central American countries . In the 1860s, Mexico was occupied by French forces , who were driven out by opponents led by the Mexican patriot Benito Juárez . Dissatisfaction with the undemocratic regime led by Porfirio Diaz led to the Mexican Revolution in 1910 . The revolutionary forces defeated the federal army, and internal friction kept the country in turmoil for more than twenty years. After the end of the revolution, the Institutional Revolutionary Party (PRI) remained in power until the end of the 20th century.

According to the constitution of February 5, 1917, Mexico is a presidential federal republic. Each of the 31 states and the federal district is sovereign in internal affairs and voluntarily united in a federation. All citizens over the age of 18, married people over the age of 16 have the right to vote. The President of the Republic is elected for 6 years and has only one mandate. He has great prestige: he is the prime minister, appoints ministers, supreme court judges and the supreme prosecutor, as well as diplomatic representatives abroad. The Federal Congress ( Congreso de la Union ) is bicameral: the House of Representatives and the Senate. The House of Representatives ( Cámara de Diputados ) has 500 members, elected for three years. The Senate ( Cámara de Senadores ) has 128 members, 4 from each state and district, elected for 6 years. Federal states have their own constitutions and parliament, they are led by governors who are elected for 6 years, members of parliament have a three-year term. The largest cities in Mexico, all Megacities , are Ciudad de Mexico, Guadalajara, Monterrey, Ecatepec, Puebla, Nezahualcóyotl, Ciudad Juarez, Tijuana, Leon and Zapopan. They are mostly located inland, coastal areas are sparsely populated. The metropolitan area of the capital has 18% of the total population of Mexico. It is also an economic center that combines around a third of the service and trade sectors and consumes two thirds of the resources for itself. Two-thirds of Mexico's higher education budget and three-quarters of its research budget are invested in the capital city. [5]
